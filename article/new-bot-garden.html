Visit our new botanical garden

Date-time Author

Step into a world of vibrant greenery and stunning blooms at the Nature’s Wonders botanical garden exhibit! This captivating new addition to the Community Science Museum celebrates the beauty and diversity of plant life, offering an immersive experience for nature enthusiasts of all ages. With lush displays, interactive learning stations, and hands-on activities, this exhibit is a must-see for anyone curious about the natural world.

Highlights of the exhibit include a tropical rainforest walkthrough, an interactive pollination garden buzzing with activity, and a fascinating showcase of carnivorous plants. Visitors can learn how plants adapt to extreme environments, discover the secrets of seed dispersal, and even pot their own mini succulents to take home. Special guided tours and workshops will also be available to deepen your understanding of the incredible plant kingdom.

The Nature’s Wonders exhibit opens on May 15, 2024, and will run through August 31, 2024. Perfect for families, school groups, or solo explorers, this exhibit offers a refreshing way to connect with nature and uncover the science behind the plants we see every day.
