Space Exhibition - Free entry

Date-time Author

Get ready to embark on an interstellar adventure at the upcoming Journey to the Stars exhibition! This immersive event brings the wonders of the universe to life, featuring cutting-edge displays, interactive exhibits, and hands-on activities. From the origins of the cosmos to the latest advances in space exploration, this exhibition offers a unique opportunity to explore the final frontier like never before.

Highlights include a life-sized model of the Mars rover, a walk-through simulation of a space station, and dazzling views of the universe in our state-of-the-art planetarium. Visitors will also experience a virtual reality journey through the Milky Way and learn about the mysteries of black holes, exoplanets, and the search for alien life. The exhibition is designed to captivate visitors of all ages, with special activities for kids, including DIY rocket launches and astronaut training challenges.

Don’t miss this chance to ignite your curiosity and discover the science and technology shaping our future among the stars. The Journey to the Stars exhibition opens on February 10, 2024, at the Community Science Museum and runs until April 28, 2024.
